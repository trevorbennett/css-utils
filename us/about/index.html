About


Trevor Bennett is a software developer, podcast host, volunteer, and small business owner. He has worked as a full stack software engineer as a part of JPMorgan Chase and Co, since 2014. In June of 2016, for want of an existing podcast, Trevor and his friend Eric launched their own podcast that focuses on central Ohio news, called Columbus This Week. This podcast as of 2018 has seen over 5,000 unique downloads across all episodes.


Trevor also is active in the central Ohio community as a software and IT volunteer. He has previously partnered with the Lebron James Family Foundation and the Groundwork Group to build out software solutions to support the goals of these non-profits. He is also currently working pro bono with Make-A-Wish Ohio, Kentucky and Indiana to build out and migrate the organization to a scalable customer relationship management platform.
